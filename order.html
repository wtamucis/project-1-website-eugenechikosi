Place an Order

Use this form to send a pastry order request to Gina's Pastries. We will review your details and contact you to confirm availability, pricing, and pickup or delivery options.

Please include as much information as you can, such as the date you need the pastries, how many people you are serving, and any flavor preferences or dietary notes.

This is a sample form for a class project. Do not enter any real payment information.

Delivery Options

  • Pickup: Collect your pastries from our shop location.
  • Standard Delivery: Delivery within 10 miles — small fee applies.
  • Express Delivery: Delivery within 2 hours — additional fee applies.
